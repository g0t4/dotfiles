# Vim Mnemonics

*Note, I prefer either a single word (i.e. `y`ank) or a short phrase that rhymes*


- `A`ppend at the End
- `I`nsert at the Start
- `J`oin (lines)
- `c`hange
- `d`elete
- `y`ank
- `t`ill / un`t`il
- `f`ind
- move/select
  - `b`ack a word
  - `w`ord / for`w`ard
  - `e`nd of word
  - `i`nner / `a`aroud
